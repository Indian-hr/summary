Payment of Bonus Act 1965 Provides statutory annual bonus linked with profits or productivity. Factories and establishments employing 20 or more employees. Employees earning ≤ ₹21,000 per month and working at least 30 days. Calculation ceiling ₹7,000 or Minimum Wage whichever higher. 30 working days in accounting year. Annual statutory bonus. No contribution system. 8.33% of annual salary. 20% of annual salary. Within 8 months of end of accounting year. Form A, B, C, D. Fraud, theft, violent misconduct. 6 months imprisonment or ₹1000 fine. Set-on and set-off allowed for 4 years.
EPF Act 1952 Provides retirement savings through provident fund. Establishments employing 20 or more employees. Employees earning ≤ ₹15,000 basic wage. ₹15,000 wage ceiling. No minimum service. Provident fund, pension, insurance. Employer 12% + Employee 12%. Mandatory contribution. No maximum PF accumulation. Monthly contribution deposit before 15th. Form 2, Form 5, Form 10, PF Register. Not applicable unless employee withdraws. Interest and penalties for late deposit. Includes EPS and EDLI benefits.
ESIC Act 1948 Provides medical, sickness, maternity and disability benefits. Establishments employing 10 or more employees. Employees earning ≤ ₹21,000 monthly. ₹21,000 wage ceiling. No minimum service. Medical insurance and cash benefits. Employer 3.25% + Employee 0.75%. Medical benefits from day one. No limit on medical treatment. Contribution paid monthly. Accident register, ESIC register. Fraudulent claims. Fine and prosecution. Includes sickness benefit, maternity benefit, disablement benefit.
Payment of Gratuity Act 1972 Provides lump sum payment for long service. Establishments employing 10 or more employees. Employees completing 5 years continuous service. No salary eligibility ceiling. 5 years continuous service. Retirement gratuity. No monthly contribution. 15 days wages per completed year. ₹20 lakh maximum. Pay within 30 days of separation. Form F, I, J, K, L. Dismissal for moral turpitude. Penalty and imprisonment. Continuous service rule applies.
ESIC Act 1948 Social security and medical care. Factories and establishments with 10+ employees. Employees earning ≤ ₹21,000. ₹21,000 monthly wage ceiling. Immediate eligibility. Medical + cash benefits. Employer 3.25% + Employee 0.75%. Sickness benefit. Lifetime medical care in some cases. Monthly contribution. ESIC records. Fraudulent claims. Fine and imprisonment. Coverage extends to dependents.
BOCW Act 1996 Protects welfare and safety of construction workers. Construction establishments employing 10 or more workers. Construction workers aged 18-60. No wage ceiling. 90 days work in year for welfare board registration. Worker welfare schemes. Cess 1-2% of construction cost. Access to welfare board benefits. Various welfare benefits. Cess payment during construction project. Worker register, safety records. Violation of safety rules. Fine and prosecution. Mandatory project registration.
Contract Labour Act 1970 Regulates employment of contract labour. Establishments employing 20 contract workers. Contract workers hired through contractor. No salary ceiling. No minimum service. Regulation of working conditions. No statutory contribution. Equal wages rule. Not applicable. License and registration required. Contractor registers and muster rolls. License violation. Fine and imprisonment. Principal employer responsible for compliance.
Shops and Establishment Act Regulates working hours and employment conditions in shops and offices. All shops, offices and commercial establishments. All employees working in commercial establishments. No salary limit. Not applicable. Working hour protection. No contribution. Weekly holidays. Not applicable. Registration within prescribed period. Attendance register. Violation of working hours rules. Fine imposed by labour department. State specific rules.
Labour Welfare Fund Provides welfare benefits for workers. Applicable establishments depending on state law. Employees earning wages within state limits. State dependent threshold. Not applicable. Worker welfare schemes. Employer + employee contribution. Small periodic contributions. Not applicable. Contribution frequency varies by state. LWF registers. Non contribution. Fine as per state rules. State administered welfare schemes.
