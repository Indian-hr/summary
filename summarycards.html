Applicable to All Establishments
Shops and Establishments Act
Regulates working conditions in shops and offices
Objective: Regulates working hours, leave, wages, holidays and employment conditions.
Establishment Eligibility: All shops, commercial establishments and offices.
Employee Eligibility: All employees working in establishments.
Salary Threshold: Not applicable.
Minimum Service: Not applicable.
Benefits: Weekly off, leave entitlement, regulated working hours.
Contribution: None.
Registers: Attendance register, wage register, leave register.
Returns: As per state rules.
Penalties: Monetary penalties for violations.
Key Highlights: Mandatory establishment registration.
Labour Welfare Fund
State welfare scheme
Objective: Provides social welfare schemes to workers.
Eligibility: Depends on state labour welfare act.
Employee Eligibility: Employees earning wages under prescribed limits.
Contribution: Employer + Employee contribution.
Rate: State specific.
Timeline: Half yearly or yearly contribution.
Registers: LWF deduction register.
Returns: State specific returns.
Penalties: Fine for non contribution.
Highlights: Funds used for education and welfare schemes.
Acts Applicable for 10+ Employees
ESIC Act
Employee health insurance
Objective: Provides medical and social security benefits.
Establishment Eligibility: Factories and establishments with 10+ employees.
Employee Eligibility: Employees earning ≤ ₹21,000.
Contribution Rate: Employer 3.25%, Employee 0.75%.
Benefits: Medical, maternity, sickness, disablement benefits.
Registers: Accident register, ESIC register.
Returns: Monthly contribution filings.
Penalties: Interest, damages and prosecution.
Highlights: Covers employees and dependents.
Gratuity Act
Retirement benefit for long service
Objective: Provides lump sum payment after long service.
Establishment Eligibility: 10+ employees.
Employee Eligibility: Minimum 5 years continuous service.
Benefit: 15 days wages per completed year.
Maximum: ₹20 lakh.
Registers: Form F, I, J, K.
Returns: As prescribed by labour department.
Penalty: Fine and imprisonment.
Highlights: Payable on resignation, retirement, death.
Acts Applicable for 20+ Employees
EPF Act
Provident fund and pension scheme
Objective: Retirement savings and pension benefits.
Establishment Eligibility: 20+ employees.
Employee Eligibility: Salary ≤ ₹15,000.
Contribution: Employer 12%, Employee 12%.
Benefits: PF savings, pension (EPS), insurance (EDLI).
Registers: Form 2, PF registers.
Returns: Monthly ECR filing.
Penalties: Interest and damages.
Highlights: Mandatory deposit before 15th.
Bonus Act
Profit sharing bonus
Eligibility: Establishments with 20+ employees.
Employee Limit: Salary ≤ ₹21,000.
Minimum Bonus: 8.33%
Maximum Bonus: 20%
Registers: Form A, B, C.
Timeline: Payment within 8 months.
Penalty: Fine or imprisonment.
Highlights: Set on and set off mechanism.
Contract Labour Act
Regulates contract employment
Eligibility: Establishments employing 20 contract workers.
Requirement: Principal employer registration and contractor license.
Registers: Muster roll, wage register.
Returns: Periodic returns.
Penalty: Fine and imprisonment.
Highlights: Principal employer responsible for compliance.
BOCW Act
Construction worker welfare
Eligibility: Construction establishments employing 10+ workers.
Contribution: 1–2% cess on project cost.
Benefit: Welfare board schemes.
Registers: Worker safety records.
Penalty: Fine and prosecution.
Highlights: Mandatory project registration.
